Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients:

    - 500g chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
    - 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
    - 1 onion, finely chopped
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
    - 1 teaspoon ground cumin
    - 1 teaspoon ground coriander
    - 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    - 1/2 teaspoon paprika
    - 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
    - Salt and pepper to taste
    - 200ml tomato puree (or tomato sauce)
    - 200ml heavy cream or coconut milk
    - Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
    - Cooked rice or naan bread (for serving)

Instructions:

    1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add chopped onion and saut√© until softened.
    2. Add minced garlic and ginger to the pan, cook for another minute until fragrant.
    3. Stir in ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric powder, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. 
       Cook for 1-2 minutes until spices are fragrant.
    4. Add chicken pieces to the pan, cook until browned on all sides.
    5. Pour in tomato puree (or tomato sauce) and stir to combine with the chicken and spices.
    6. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until chicken is cooked through and tender.
    7. Stir in heavy cream (or coconut milk) and simmer for another 5 minutes to heat through and thicken the sauce.
    8. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
    9. Serve Chicken Tikka Masala hot, garnished with fresh cilantro, alongside rice or naan bread.

Enjoy your flavorful Chicken Tikka Masala!
