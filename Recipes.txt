### **Italian Cuisine: Spaghetti Carbonara**

**Ingredients:**
- 200g spaghetti
- 100g pancetta
- 2 large eggs
- 50g pecorino cheese, grated
- 50g parmesan cheese, grated
- 2 cloves garlic, peeled and crushed
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and black pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente.
2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil and add the pancetta. Cook until crispy.
3. Add the garlic to the pan and cook for a minute, then remove the garlic.
4. Beat the eggs in a bowl and mix in the cheeses.
5. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the pan with the pancetta. Remove from heat.
6. Quickly add the egg and cheese mixture, stirring to create a creamy sauce. 
7. Season with salt and black pepper to taste and serve immediately.

### **Mexican Cuisine: Chicken Tacos**

**Ingredients:**
- 500g chicken breast, cut into strips
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 8 small flour tortillas
- Fresh cilantro, chopped
- Lime wedges for serving

**Instructions:**
1. Heat the olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
2. Add the chicken strips to the pan and cook until browned.
3. Stir in the chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook for another 5 minutes.
4. Warm the tortillas in a dry pan or microwave.
5. Fill the tortillas with the chicken mixture, and top with fresh cilantro.
6. Serve with lime wedges on the side.

### **Japanese Cuisine: Teriyaki Salmon**

**Ingredients:**
- 4 salmon fillets
- 1/2 cup soy sauce
- 1/4 cup mirin (sweet rice wine)
- 1/4 cup sake
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- 1 tbsp vegetable oil
- 2 green onions, chopped
- Sesame seeds for garnish

**Instructions:**
1. In a bowl, mix the soy sauce, mirin, sake, and brown sugar.
2. Marinate the salmon fillets in the mixture for at least 30 minutes.
3. Heat the vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat. Remove the salmon from the marinade and cook until browned and cooked through.
4. In a separate pan, heat the marinade until it thickens slightly.
5. Serve the salmon with the sauce poured over it, garnished with green onions and sesame seeds.

### **Chicken Tikka Masala**

**Ingredients:**
- 500g chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1/2 teaspoon paprika
- 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 200ml tomato puree (or tomato sauce)
- 200ml heavy cream or coconut milk
- Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
- Cooked rice or naan bread (for serving)

**Instructions:**
1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add chopped onion and sauté until softened.
2. Add minced garlic and ginger to the pan, cook for another minute until fragrant.
3. Stir in ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric powder, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Cook for 1-2 minutes until spices are fragrant.
4. Add chicken pieces to the pan, cook until browned on all sides.
5. Pour in tomato puree (or tomato sauce) and stir to combine with the chicken and spices.
6. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until chicken is cooked through and tender.
7. Stir in heavy cream (or coconut milk) and simmer for another 5 minutes to heat through and thicken the sauce.
8. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
9. Serve Chicken Tikka Masala hot, garnished with fresh cilantro, alongside rice or naan bread.

Enjoy your flavorful Chicken Tikka Masala!

### **Classic Cheeseburger with Fries**

**Ingredients for Cheeseburger:**
- 500g ground beef (preferably 80% lean)
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 4 hamburger buns
- 4 slices of cheese (cheddar, American, or your choice)
- Burger toppings (lettuce, tomato slices, onion slices, pickles, etc.)
- Condiments (ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, etc.)

**Ingredients for Fries:**
- 4 large potatoes, peeled (optional) and cut into fries
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Optional: paprika, garlic powder, or other seasonings

**Instructions:**

1. **Preparation for Fries:**
   - Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
   - In a large bowl, toss potato fries with vegetable oil, salt, pepper, and any optional seasonings until evenly coated.
   - Arrange fries in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or aluminum foil.
   - Bake for 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway through, until fries are golden and crispy.

2. **Preparation for Cheeseburgers:**
   - Divide ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape into patties slightly larger than the burger buns. Season both sides generously with salt and pepper.
   - Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook patties for 3-4 minutes per side, or until desired doneness. In the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheese on each patty to melt.
   - Toast burger buns lightly on the grill or in a toaster.

3. **Assembling the Cheeseburgers:**
   - Spread condiments (ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise) on the bottom half of each burger bun.
   - Place lettuce, tomato slices, onion slices, and pickles on top of the condiments.
   - Place a cheeseburger patty with melted cheese on top of the vegetables.
   - Close the burger with the top half of the burger bun.

4. **Serving:**
   - Serve the cheeseburgers hot with the crispy fries on the side.
   - Enjoy your classic cheeseburger and fries!
