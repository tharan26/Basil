Teriyaki Salmon

Ingredients:

    - 4 salmon fillets
    - 1/2 cup soy sauce
    - 1/4 cup mirin (sweet rice wine)
    - 1/4 cup sake
    - 2 tbsp brown sugar
    - 1 tbsp vegetable oil
    - 2 green onions, chopped
    - Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:

    1. In a bowl, mix the soy sauce, mirin, sake, and brown sugar.
    2. Marinate the salmon fillets in the mixture for at least 30 minutes.
    3. Heat the vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat. Remove the salmon from the marinade and cook until browned and cooked through.
    4. In a separate pan, heat the marinade until it thickens slightly.
    5. Serve the salmon with the sauce poured over it, garnished with green onions and sesame seeds.


Enjoy your flavorful Teriyaki Salmon!
